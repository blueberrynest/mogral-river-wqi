# WQI Calculation Tables and Results

> Extracted from: *Evaluation of Water Quality Index for River Mogral (2021)*
> Source pages (PDF): 41–50

The Water Quality Index (WQI) for each sampling station was calculated using the Weighted Arithmetic Water Quality Index method. The parameters considered were pH, Dissolved Oxygen (DO), Biological Oxygen Demand (BOD), Chlorides, and Alkalinity.
