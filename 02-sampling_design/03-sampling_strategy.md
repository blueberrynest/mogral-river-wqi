## Sample Collection and Analysis

Water samples were collected from twenty-four sampling stations (S1–S24) along the Mogral River.

**Sampling months**
- January
- February

**Sampling standard**
- IS: 2498 (1966) – Part I

**Parameters analyzed**
- Temperature
- pH
- Dissolved Oxygen (DO)
- Biochemical Oxygen Demand (BOD)
- Alkalinity
- Chloride

**Laboratory guidelines**
- APHA (2012) Standard Methods for Examination of Water and Wastewater
- Analytical grade chemicals and reagents were used.
