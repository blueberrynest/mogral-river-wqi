# pH Measurement

> *Extracted from: Evaluation of Water Quality Index for River Mogral (2021)*
> *Source pages (PDF): 29*

## Apparatus

* pH meter with electrodes
* Thermometer
* pH papers

## Reagents

* Buffer solutions of known pH values

## Procedure

1. Rinse the electrode with distilled water and dry it using tissue paper.
2. Immerse the electrode in a suitable buffer solution.
3. Make the necessary corrections for temperature.
4. Measure the pH and adjust the calibration knob until the reading shows the correct value.
5. Rinse the electrode with distilled water and dry it again using tissue paper.
6. Immerse the electrode in a buffer solution at the other extreme of the range and adjust the value using the correction knob.
7. Rinse the electrode again with distilled water and dry it.
8. Place the electrode in the sample whose pH is to be measured and note the reading.
9. After measurement, dip and store the electrode in 4M KCl solution.
